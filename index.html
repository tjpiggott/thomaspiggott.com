Explore
Before a single sketch gets drawn, I must truly know my user. It's my job to understand, and beyond that, empathize with the people I design for. That means getting out into the field, observing, and asking questions to ensure no assumptions go unchecked.
Design
Design is an iterative process that allows me to try multiple solutions in order to see what works best. The entire process runs through sketches, paper prototypes, and digital wireframes for a start, all the way up to custom-built interactive prototypes and pixel-perfect photoshop mockups.
Share
No design work is done in a vacuum; The importance of sharing designs with teammates, stakeholders, and users cannot be underestimated. Working together, teaching each other, and responding to feedback are essential to a successful product.
