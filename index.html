My design process and philosophy
Research
Research is the key to ensuring you're building a solution for a problem that exists and matters.
Design
Taking research and putting it to work, iteratively. No design will be perfect the first time, that's why design requires constant adjusting as more information is learned.
Development
Development should never mean a simple handoff of the design but rather an ongoing conversation with developers to balance design needs with the constraints of development.
